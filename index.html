Encounter

About

The purpose of this project is to visualise the emergence of social networks between individuals with different personalities. This is inspired by the MBTI personality model, which categorises individuals into 16 personality types. The model suggests that individuals with a particular personality type may be attracted to or repelled by those with another personality. Based on these fundamental laws, they build, expand, and change their social networks and eventually change the trajectory of the entire social environment.

In this project, four colours of particles represent four personalities, including INFP, ESFJ, ENFJ and INFJ.Particles with different colours follow their own rules. They may attract, repel or chase each other, resulting in an intricate system. Since the MBTI is only an idealised model, real-life relationships are influenced by complex factors. The project may reveal situations contradicting MBTI assumptions, providing insight into the complexities of real-life interpersonal dynamics.

Rules

Red particles only attracts to those with the same colours.

Green particles attract to blue particles and themselves.

Purple particles repel particles of the same colour.

Blue particles dispel the green ones.
